About ICCRLAB

The ICCR Lab is a private and autonomous body promoting research based activities all over the world. The ICCR aims to do non-profit collaborative research in the field of engineering, applied sciences and management. We aim to be the leading independent academic and professional body working in collaboration with academicians, faculties, students, researchers and educational institutes. This leads us to be playing a creative and critical role in the society by disseminating teaching and research on a global scale, the cornerstones of which are good, long-term relationships, a focus on real life applications necessary for the welfare of the mankind, and an ability to combine quality and innovation.
